Why is the Sky Blue? 💙
Have you ever looked up and wondered — why is the sky blue? 🌤️
The answer is SO cool — and you are going to love it! ✨
☁️
☁️
Sunlight is full of colours! 🌈
Sunlight looks white — but it is secretly hiding ALL the colours of the rainbow inside it! 🎨

Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet — they are ALL travelling together inside sunlight!

You can see them when sunlight shines through a glass of water — a rainbow appears! 🌈
💨
Sunlight bounces in our air! 💨
When sunlight comes down from the Sun, it travels through our air ! 🌍

Air is full of tiny tiny invisible particles — so small you can never ever see them!

When sunlight hits these tiny particles, the light bounces off in all directions — like a ball bouncing everywhere! 🎱✨
💙
Blue bounces the most! 💙
Here is the magic part! Blue light loves to bounce! It bounces in every direction all over the sky!

The other colours do not bounce as much — they just keep going straight.

So when you look up, blue light is flying at your eyes from everywhere!

That is why the whole sky looks blue! 💙🎉
