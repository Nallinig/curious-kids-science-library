Why Do Animals Need Our Help? 🌍
Our planet is home to millions of amazing animals! 🐘🦁🐋🦜
But some of them are in trouble — and they need us to help! 💚
🦁
🐘
⚠️
What Does "Endangered" Mean? ⚠️
When we say an animal is endangered, it means there are only a very small number of them left in the whole world.

If we don't help them, one day there might be none left at all — and that would be called extinction. Gone forever. 😢

But here is the good news — people all around the world are working SO hard to save them! 💪🌍
🌳
🌲
🏠
Reason 1: Losing Their Home 🏠
Every animal has a home in nature — a forest, a river, an ocean, a grassland.

Sometimes people cut down forests to build houses and farms. Sometimes rivers get dirty. Sometimes grasslands disappear.

When an animal's home is destroyed, they have nowhere to live, nowhere to find food, and nowhere to have babies. 🌳💔

Protecting nature means protecting their homes!
🛡️
Reason 2: Hunting 🛡️
Some animals have been hunted by humans for a very long time — for their fur, their tusks, their horns, or their skin.

When too many animals are hunted, there are not enough left to have babies and keep the family going.

That is why today there are laws in most countries that make it illegal to hunt endangered animals. 🚫

Rangers and wildlife protectors work every day to keep animals safe! 💪
🌡️
Reason 3: Our Changing Planet 🌡️
Our planet is getting a little warmer because of pollution in the air. This is called climate change.

When temperatures change, animals that need cold weather — like polar bears and penguins — find their icy homes melting away. 🐧🐻‍❄️

Ocean animals are affected too when the water gets too warm. 🌊

Every time we save energy or plant a tree, we are helping! 🌱✨
