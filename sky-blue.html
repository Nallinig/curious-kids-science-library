Why is the Sky Blue? 💙
Have you ever looked up and wondered — why is the sky that beautiful blue colour? 🌈
It's one of the best questions EVER — and the answer is amazing science!
☀️ Sunlight 🌈 Colours 💨 Air 💙 Blue!
☁️
☁️
Sunlight is FULL of Colours! 🌈
Here's a secret — sunlight looks white or yellow, but it's actually hiding ALL the colours of the rainbow inside it!
Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet — they're ALL there, travelling together! 🎨
You can see this when sunlight shines through a glass of water or after rain — a rainbow appears and shows all the hidden colours! 🌈
💨
Light Travels Through Air 💨
When sunlight travels from the Sun down to Earth, it has to pass through our atmosphere — the layer of air that wraps around our planet like a cosy blanket! 🌍
Air is full of tiny tiny particles — so small you can never see them — called molecules.
When light hits these tiny particles, something magical happens — the light bounces and scatters in all directions! 💫
Think of it like a ball bouncing off lots of little bumpers! 🎱

But Why BLUE Specifically? 💙
Here's the cool part! Not all colours bounce the same way.
Blue light bounces around the MOST — it scatters in every direction all across the sky!
Red and orange light travel more in a straight line and don't bounce as much.
So when you look up, blue light is coming at your eyes from EVERYWHERE in the sky — that's why the whole sky looks blue! 💙
Blue is basically the most energetic, bouncy colour of all! 🎉

Why is Sunset Red and Orange? 🌅
Have you noticed the sky turns red and orange at sunset? Here's why!
When the Sun is low in the sky, sunlight has to travel through much MORE air to reach your eyes.
All the blue light gets scattered away long before it reaches you.
But red and orange light travel further without bouncing — so THEY reach your eyes at sunset!
Same sky, same Sun — but a completely different colour show! 🎨✨

Why Are Clouds White? ☁️
Great question! Clouds are made of tiny water droplets.
These droplets are much bigger than air molecules — so they scatter ALL colours of light equally, not just blue!
When all colours scatter together equally, they mix back into white light — so clouds look white! ☁️
Dark storm clouds look grey because they're so thick and heavy with water that most light can't get through! 🌧️

Other Planet Skies! 🪐
Here's something amazing — other planets have different coloured skies!
🔴 Mars has a pinkish-red sky — because its air is full of red dust!
🟡 Venus has an orange-yellow sky — thick clouds of yellow gas!
💙 Neptune has a deep blue sky — even bluer than Earth!
Our blue sky is actually special and beautiful — it means we have just the right air for us to breathe and live! 🌍💙
