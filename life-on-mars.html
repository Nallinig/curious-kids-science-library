Mars Long Ago — A Wet World! 🌊
Billions of years ago, Mars was a completely different planet!

It had rivers, lakes and possibly even oceans of liquid water! Scientists can see the dried-up riverbeds and shorelines still carved into the Martian rock today. 🪨

It also had a thicker atmosphere — a layer of air — that kept it warmer. Like Earth, but different!

Could tiny life have existed in those ancient waters? Scientists think it is possible — and they are searching for the answer RIGHT NOW! 🔬
💨
What Happened to Mars? 💨
Something dramatic happened to Mars about 3-4 billion years ago.

Mars lost its magnetic field — the invisible shield that protects a planet. Without it, the solar wind — a stream of energy from the Sun — slowly blew away most of the atmosphere! 💨

Without atmosphere, the planet got freezing cold and the water turned to ice or evaporated.

Today Mars is dry, dusty and bitterly cold — about minus 60 degrees on average! 🥶
Mars Today 🔴
Today Mars is cold, rocky and covered in red dust — that is why it looks red! The dust is iron oxide — basically rust! 🦀

But there IS water on Mars today — frozen as ice at the poles! 🧊 Massive ice caps sit at the top and bottom of Mars.

Scientists also think there may be liquid water deep underground — kept from freezing by heat from inside the planet! 🌋

Where there is water — there might be life! Even tiny microscopic life hiding underground! 🦠
🤖
The Rovers — Our Robots on Mars! 🤖
We have sent amazing robot explorers to Mars — called rovers! They drive around on the surface, taking photos and collecting rocks! 🪨📸

Perseverance is there right now — collecting rock samples that may one day be brought back to Earth to be tested for signs of ancient life! 🌍

Curiosity has been roving since 2012 and found that Mars once had all the right conditions for life!

Ingenuity — a tiny helicopter — became the first aircraft to fly on another planet! ✈️🎉
🦠
Could Tiny Life Exist on Mars NOW? 🦠
On Earth, tiny creatures called extremophiles live in the most extreme places — inside volcanoes, in frozen ice, deep underground with no sunlight! 🌋🧊

Scientists think similar tiny life might be hiding deep underground on Mars — where it is warmer and liquid water might exist! 💧

We have not found it yet — but we have not finished looking! The search is still happening! 🔬

Finding even one tiny microbe on Mars would be the greatest discovery in human history! 🌟
🚀
Could Humans Live on Mars? 🚀
This is the BIG question — and the answer is maybe, one day — but it would be VERY hard!

Mars has no breathable air — astronauts would need space suits outside always. 👨‍🚀
It is extremely cold — heated habitats would be needed. 🏠
Radiation from the Sun is dangerous without a magnetic shield. ☢️

But scientists are working on solutions — special habitats, growing food in greenhouses, making oxygen from Martian air! 🌱

SpaceX and NASA both plan to send humans to Mars in the coming decades! 🌍🔴
👨‍🚀
YOUR Generation Will Go to Mars! 👨‍🚀
Here is the most exciting part — the first humans to walk on Mars are probably alive right now! 🌟

NASA plans to send astronauts to Mars in the 2030s and 2040s — when you could be a young adult! 🚀

The scientists, engineers and astronauts who will make it happen are studying science right now — just like you! 📚
